Airdata UAV is the industry standard for drone fleet management. It automatically syncs flight logs from DJI, Autel, and other drones, providing detailed analytics on battery health, motor performance, and flight patterns. The dashboard offers predictive maintenance alerts based on manufacturer thresholds. Integration with Zapier enables seamless workflow automation.
