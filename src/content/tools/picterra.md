Picterra enables wildlife biologists to detect animals in drone imagery without coding. Upload orthomosaics, train a custom detector with a few annotations, and run inference. The platform handles large datasets and exports results as GeoJSON. It's accurate and user-friendly, making AI accessible for field biologists.
