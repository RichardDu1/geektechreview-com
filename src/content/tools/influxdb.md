InfluxDB is the backbone for storing the continuous stream of sensor readings and anomaly scores. Its SQL-like query language (Flux) makes it easy to aggregate data for Grafana dashboards. The free tier is enough for a single installation, but for multiple clients, consider the paid plan. It's reliable and integrates seamlessly with Grafana.
