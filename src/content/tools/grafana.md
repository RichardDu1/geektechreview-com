Grafana turns raw InfluxDB data into actionable insights. You can build a dashboard showing real-time anomaly scores, temperature trends, and audio waveforms. Set up alert rules to get notified via email or Slack when the anomaly score exceeds a threshold. It's the final piece that makes predictive maintenance practical for busy installers.
