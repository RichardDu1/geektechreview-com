Cropwise is a comprehensive farm management platform that helps consultants create professional crop health reports. It allows importing of drone and satellite data, overlaying field boundaries, and generating customizable PDF reports with charts and recommendations. The platform also includes weather data and pest models to enrich analysis. Its intuitive interface makes it easy to share insights with clients.
