Edge Impulse simplifies the process of creating custom anomaly detection models. You upload time-series data (current, temperature, audio), and the platform automatically extracts features and trains a model. The free tier is sufficient for a single amplifier setup. Deployment to the Raspberry Pi is a one-click download. It's the easiest way to get AI-powered predictive maintenance without deep learning expertise.
